What is KSA?

The Tufts Korean Students Assocation, or KSA, provides students of all backgrounds an opportunity to learn, experience, and celebrate Korean culture. To accomplish this task, Tufts KSA holds many exciting events throughout the year to bring a diverse community of students at Tufts University together.

We hope that you enjoy your wonderful years as Jumbos and also that you join us in the many events to come!
