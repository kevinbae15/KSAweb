The KSA E-board elections are held at the end of every Spring semester, and Freshmen Representative elections are held at the beginning of every Fall semester. Anyone with an interest in Korean culture, regardless of ethnicity, political affiliation, identity or religion, is welcome to attend! If interested, join our e-list for more information!
